Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic game that is played by two players taking turns placing X's and O's in a 3x3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. If all 9 cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a tie.

How to Play

  1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  2. Player 1 (X) goes first and places an "X" on any empty cell.
  3. Player 2 (O) goes next and places an "O" on any empty cell.
  4. Players take turns until one player gets three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally), or all 9 cells are filled.
  5. If a player gets three in a row, they win the game.
  6. If all 9 cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a tie.
  7. Click the "New Game" button to start a new game.
